Services & Offerings

Offer 1

Modernization & Reliability Assessment

A two-week assessment that identifies the safest modernization path without disrupting operations.

For

Manufacturing plants and enterprise IT teams with legacy MES/ERP integrations, brittle data flows, slow releases, and recurring production-impacting incidents.

Outcome

A clear, prioritized plan to modernize safely without disrupting day-to-day operations.

Included

  • Architecture and integration review across MES, ERP, SCADA, IoT gateways, APIs, and batch jobs
  • Data flow review across stream and batch pipelines, historians, warehouses, and quality data
  • Performance and availability risk mapping, including bottlenecks and single points of failure
  • Observability gap analysis across metrics, logs, traces, alerts, and incident readiness
  • CI/CD and release process review, including test coverage, environments, and rollback strategy
  • Security basics review covering access patterns, secrets handling, and audit/logging readiness

Offer 2

Delivery Acceleration Sprint

A four-to-six week sprint that ships one production-ready modernization slice while establishing safer delivery practices.

For

Teams that need a fast, tangible improvement with minimal operational risk and a clear path to production.

Outcome

Ship a modernization slice and establish a practical golden path for ongoing delivery.

Included

  • Refactor or extract one critical service or module using a strangler approach
  • Stabilize one key pipeline such as telemetry, quality data, or production reporting
  • Add parity and integration tests with safer release controls such as feature flags
  • Implement structured logging, trace correlation, metrics, dashboards, and alerting for critical flows
  • Improve latency, reduce data-loss risk, and prepare a production rollout plan with rollback safety
  • Establish a CI/CD baseline for build, test, and security checks around the selected slice
  • Create operational handover notes so the team can support the new flow after rollout
  • Define a practical golden path that can be reused for the next modernization slice

Offer 3

Managed Engineering Retainer

A monthly retained model for continuous stability, incremental modernization, and steady operational improvement.

For

Plants and enterprise IT teams that need continuous stability and modernization without building a large in-house platform team.

Outcome

Predictable delivery, reduced incidents, and ongoing modernization through a structured operating cadence.

Included

  • Production support during business hours, including bug fixes and minor enhancements
  • Monthly health reporting covering KPIs, operational risks, and prioritized backlog items
  • Incremental modernization work, including one to two focused epics per month where appropriate
  • Performance and cost optimization, plus proactive reliability work such as SLO tuning and incident prevention
  • AI workflow pilots integrated with core systems, including SOP search, incident summaries, and ticket triage where relevant
  • Evaluation and guardrails for grounded answers, access control, and quarterly architecture roadmap refresh

Frequently asked questions

How do you approach core engineering modernization safely?

We use phased delivery, test harnesses, migration sequencing, and controlled cutovers so backend and data changes remain reversible, measurable, and operationally safe.

Do you build AI chatbots?

Our focus is on AI embedded in internal systems and workflows, supported by retrieval, permissions, auditability, and clearly defined operating metrics.

What does the managed reliability retainer cover?

The retainer typically includes monthly support, targeted enhancements, observability, performance and cost tuning, incident reduction, and monitoring for AI-enabled workflows.

How do you measure success?

We define success measures at the outset, including release confidence, latency, incident rate, support load, automation gains, and business outcomes aligned to the engagement.
